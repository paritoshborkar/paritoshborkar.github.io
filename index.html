Purpose of the Platform

I have done my Bachelor's in Engineering Physics and my Master's in Computer Science. Despite spending most of my life studying in these institutions, I don't feel that I have any significant work to show for it. I wasn't motivated to study out of pure curiosity and passion for these subjects or with a concrete goal in mind. I went with the flow and instead of challenging myself to question the things I was learning in school I merely sat back and soaked in whatever was thrown at me.

With thousands of interesting blogs on the internet made by college students and working professionals, what value will my platform provide to anyone? The answer is pretty simple. This is not meant to provide value to anyone else but me. I wanted to create a way to document everything I have learnt and will learn during my years in academia and my professional life in the future. Sure I could simply maintain a personal diary, whether physical or digital. But I want to create a body of work that documents the stuff that learnt during my years in academia simply for the joy of documenting my knowledge base and in the process teach myself to learn better. A personal website seemed like the perfect way to do just that.
